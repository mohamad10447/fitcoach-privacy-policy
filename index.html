Privacy Policy for FitCoach

Last updated: May 26, 2026

FitCoach (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

FitCoach may collect the following types of information:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (features used, interactions, crash reports)
  • Fitness-related data you choose to enter (such as workouts or activity logs)

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, passwords, or government identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand app usage trends

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We may share limited technical data with trusted third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting tools) solely for improving app performance. These providers are required to protect your data.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

FitCoach may use third-party services such as analytics and crash reporting tools. These services may collect non-personal usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

FitCoach is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, or delete your personal data. You can contact us to make such requests.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app or on this page.
